Departure Briefing

Change as necessary, for the current day & runway in use.
Make inital call to LYDIA to get the preferrable runway before doing it so make initial radio call first

> Today is going to be a VFR departure using Runway 13/31 to Training Area via Kalamon

> We expect to taxi right, left, then via Hotel & Foxtrot to Holding Point Alpha/Bravo

> Our speeds today will be 54 V_rotate, 76 Initial Climb, & 86 MCP Climb

> In case of abnormal indications before V_r, the captain willl call STOP, throttle to idle, and apply brakes as necessary to stop

> In case of an emergency after V_r with sufficient runway remaining, the captain will call LAND, pitch down as necessary, and apply brakes as necessary to stop on the runway.

> In the event of an emergency after V_r with insufficient runway remaining, the captain will call land, we will glide at best glide speed, and look for a field within 30degrees of the runway heading using flaps as necessary.

> In a flyable emergency we will join the downwind and land back at the aerodrome

> In all real emergencys the captain will take controls and communications.

> The runway today is dry/wet and we will plan accordingly

> Any questions?

> Departure briefing complete

Arrival Briefing

Make initial radio call to Lydia Radio when switching from Kavala Tower to get runway in use. Consider most logical entry then make the arrival briefing

> This is going to be a VFR arrival to Lydia via KALAMON/RUPOUL/SYLLA via IP13 at 1700'

> We expect an OVERHEAD/DEADSIDE entry for LEFT/RIGHT circuit to RUNWAY 13/31

> We will fly the circuit at traffic pattern altitude of 1000'/1200' and will be targetting a speed of 81kt IAS on base and 60 on final.

> Today we expect to correct for LEFT/RIGHT X-Wind (if applicable)

> Any questions?

Radio Calls

Order of the most common radio calls I've made. Sometimes some especially to Kavala are skipped if they ask to report later or you can't get on the radio and be heard. Write down what time/location they want you to report. Don't forget to shorten the call-sign if LYDIA or KAVALA does first.

> Initial Call to LYDIA RADIO
Lydia Radio, good morning, F-HXCD on Stand 22, radio check and confirm you have received our flight plan.

> Before Taxi LYDIA RADIO
F-HXCD Taxi Instructions

> Holding Short to LYDIA RADIO
F-HXCD ready for departure/ready for backtrack and departure (depending on RWY 13/31)

> Downwind Call to LYDIA RADIO
F-HXCD established RIGHT/LEFT downwind RWY 13/31 climbing to 3000 overhead the station.

> Final Call to LYDIA RADIO
F-HXCD switching to Kavala

> Initial Call to KAVALA APPROACH
Kavala approach, good morning, F-HXCD overhead Lydia at 3000 requesting 4500 to KALAMON for ALISTRATI TRAINING AREA

> Overhead Kalamon or Entering Alistrati to KAVALA APPROACH
F-HXCD entering ALISTRATI area at 4500' remain at the area from 4500' to VFR minima for 15 minutes

> Leaving Training Area to KAVALA APPROACH
F-HXCD leaving ALISTRATI area at 4000' request rejoin to LYDIA via KALAMON 2000' at KALAMON 25(time)

> Final Call to KAVALA APPROACH
F-HXCD overhead Kalamon switching to Lydia

> Initial Call to LYDIA RADIO
Lydia Radio, F-HXCD hello again, we are overhead KALAMON at 2000 towards IP13

> IP13 Call to LYDIA RADIO
F-HXCD over IP13 1700' for OVERHEAD/DOWNWIND entry to RWY 13/31 for FULL STOP/TOUCH & GO

> Downwind Call to LYDIA RADIO
F-HXCD on LEFT/RIGHT downwind RWY 13/31 for FULL STOP/TOUCH & GO

> Base Call to LYDIA RADIO
F-HXCD on LEFT/RIGHT base RWY 13/31 for FULL STOP/TOUCH & GO

> Final Call to LYDIA RADIO
F-HXCD final RWY 13/31 for FULL STOP/TOUCH & GO

> Vacated to LYDIA RADIO
F-HXCD vacated via A/B/C

> Final Call to LYDIA RADIO
F-HXCD shutting down

• IN-FLIGHT CHECKS

Essential items to verify while airborne

LEFT (Every 1000')

  • Lookout
  • Engine (Parameteres in the Green)
  • Fuel (Sufficient)
  • Temperature (EGT)

FREDA (Every 15')

  • Fuel (Sufficient)
  • Radio (Still on correct frequency)
  • Engine Instruments (All Green)
  • Direction (Check and set heading bug too if necessary)
  • Altitude (On assigned)

HASELL (For some exercises)

  • Height (min 3000' AGL)
  • Airframe (Flaps, trim...)
  • Security (Nothing Loose, seatbelts fastened)
  • Engine (All parameters green)
  • Location (Not over city, inside training area)
  • Lookout (2 x 90° or 1 x 180° turn

CASHIF (Downwind)

  • Clearance (make call)
  • Airframe (Landing light on)
  • Security (Nothing loose, seatbelts on)
  • Height (1000' or 1200' depending on RWY)
  • Instruments (All Green)
  • Fuel (Sufficient)

CASH (On Final)

  • Clearance (make call)
  • Airframe (Flaps LDG)
  • Speed (60 KIAS)
  • Height (Check PAPI or known points)
